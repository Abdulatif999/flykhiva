Over the past period, significant reforms have been carried out in all sectors of the republic's economy, including consistent measures for the development of the country's civil aviation. In particular, for the development of this sphere, a number of acts of our esteemed President have been adopted aimed at improving civil aviation and transforming and supporting civil aviation enterprises.

Inspired by these reforms and in order to contribute to the development of this industry, at the beginning of 2021 We have established the company “Fly Khiva Group“ LLC (ID 935828) in the city of Khiva in order to develop civil, cargo and business aviation, performing both domestic and international flights. On October 15 last year at the Ministry of Transport of the Republic of Uzbekistan, representatives of the company “Fly Khiva Group”, a presentation of the concept of organizing local flights based on IL-114-100 aircraft was held.

The company “Fly Khiva Group” has offices in the cities of Khiva and Tashkent, in addition, representatives in Moscow, in St. Petersburg. Also, the company organizes passenger charter flights to various destinations and cargo charter flights from China to Uzbekistan and Russia
